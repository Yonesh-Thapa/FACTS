5-Week Job-Ready Training Program

Comprehensive accounting skills and career development for recent graduates

Program Overview

Our comprehensive 5-week program is specifically designed for recent accounting graduates who are looking to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical workplace skills.

The F.A.C.T.S training program combines technical accounting software skills with essential workplace competencies that employers are looking for. Our instructor-led live sessions provide hands-on training with real-world applications, helping you develop the confidence and competence needed to excel in your first accounting role.

Weekly Curriculum

A structured approach to building your skills over 5 weeks

Week 1: Xero & MYOB Basics

Topics Covered:

  • Xero navigation and setup
  • MYOB AccountRight fundamentals
  • Chart of accounts and organization
  • Creating and processing invoices
  • Account reconciliation basics

Outcomes:

By the end of Week 1, you will be comfortable navigating both Xero and MYOB platforms and performing basic accounting tasks in each system.

Week 2: Bank Reconciliation & Payroll

Topics Covered:

  • Advanced bank reconciliation techniques
  • Managing exceptions and discrepancies
  • Payroll setup and configuration
  • Processing pay runs and payroll taxes
  • Superannuation management

Outcomes:

After Week 2, you will be able to effectively manage bank reconciliations and set up and process payroll, including understanding Australian payroll tax requirements.

Week 3: BAS & Tax Essentials

Topics Covered:

  • GST fundamentals and calculations
  • BAS preparation and lodgment
  • Common tax compliance issues
  • Record-keeping requirements
  • End of financial year processes

Outcomes:

By the end of Week 3, you will understand Australian GST requirements, be able to prepare Business Activity Statements, and navigate essential tax compliance requirements.

Week 4: Resume & LinkedIn Optimization

Topics Covered:

  • Accounting-specific resume formatting
  • Highlighting relevant skills and experience
  • Creating an optimized LinkedIn profile
  • Networking strategies for accounting professionals
  • Job search techniques for accounting graduates

Outcomes:

After Week 4, you will have a professionally crafted resume and LinkedIn profile that effectively highlights your accounting education and newly acquired practical skills.

Week 5: Interview Practice & Workplace Etiquette

Topics Covered:

  • Common accounting interview questions
  • Mock interview practice sessions
  • Professional communication skills
  • Workplace expectations and etiquette
  • Continuing professional development planning

Outcomes:

By the end of Week 5, you will be prepared to excel in accounting job interviews and understand how to navigate professional workplace environments successfully.

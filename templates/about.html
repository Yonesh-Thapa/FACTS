About F.A.C.T.S

Led by Darshan Kumar Thapa, ASA CPA, MPAS, Xero Certified Consultant

Our Mission

F.A.C.T.S is dedicated to empowering aspiring accountants by bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world practice. We provide practical, job-ready training and mentorship, enabling our students to confidently launch and grow their accounting careers.

Our Vision

To build a future where every accounting graduate enters the workforce with confidence, practical experience, and a professional network. We envision F.A.C.T.S as a leading platform that produces skilled accountants and fosters a community of continuous learning and career advancement.

What We Stand For

Practical Learning

We believe that practical, hands-on experience is essential for mastering accounting skills. Our training focuses on real-world applications rather than just theoretical knowledge.

Mentorship & Personal Attention

With limited class sizes, we ensure every student receives personalized guidance. We're committed to understanding each student's unique needs and career goals.

Integrity & Professionalism

We uphold the highest standards of professional ethics and integrity in all aspects of our training, preparing students to maintain these values throughout their careers.

Innovation & Future-Readiness

We continuously update our curriculum to incorporate emerging technologies and trends, ensuring our graduates are prepared for the evolving landscape of accounting.

Student Success

Our ultimate measure of success is the achievement of our students. We're dedicated to providing the knowledge, skills, and support needed for our graduates to excel in their accounting careers.

Why Choose F.A.C.T.S

Industry Expert Instructor

Learn directly from Darshan Kumar Thapa, an ASA CPA and Xero Advisor Certified professional with broad experience in Australian accounting—across public practice, cloud accounting systems, tax compliance, financial reporting, and client advisory services.

Practical, Hands-on Training

Our program emphasizes real-world skills including Xero, MYOB, Accounts Receivable Cycle, Accounts Payable, Bank Reconciliation (including credit card reconciliation), Payroll Processing, GST, and BAS Preparation.

Job Readiness Focus

We don't just teach accounting software – we prepare you for the entire job search process with resume building, LinkedIn optimization, and interview practice.

Small Class Sizes

With a maximum of 10 students per session, you'll receive personalized attention and have your specific questions answered.

100% Online Delivery

Join from anywhere in Australia without worrying about travel or relocation. All classes are conducted live online with interactive sessions.

Completion Certificate

Receive a certificate of completion to add to your resume, showing potential employers your commitment to professional development.

Our Story

F.A.C.T.S was founded by Darshan Kumar Thapa after recognizing a significant gap in the Australian accounting education system. While many graduates leave university with strong theoretical knowledge, they often lack the practical, software-based skills that employers seek in entry-level accountants. The program also develops essential soft skills such as communication, adaptability, and teamwork to support professional growth.

Drawing on his experience as both an accountant and educator, Darshan developed a comprehensive 8-week program specifically designed to bridge this gap and help recent graduates secure their first accounting roles.

With his extensive background in public practice accounting, cloud accounting solutions, tax compliance, and client advisory services, Darshan brings real-world expertise directly to his students. His approachable teaching style and passion for mentoring have made him highly regarded among graduates seeking to enhance their employability in Australia's competitive accounting job market.

Darshan personally guides each cohort through the entire 8-week program, providing hands-on instruction and mentorship. His teaching philosophy emphasizes practical skills that directly translate to workplace success, ensuring students not only understand accounting concepts but can apply them effectively in professional settings.

FACTS is committed to helping accounting graduates succeed. Our training not only builds technical expertise but also emphasizes soft skills that are critical in today's job market.

Our approach combines technical training with career development, ensuring that our graduates are not only proficient in industry-standard accounting software but also prepared to navigate the job search process effectively.

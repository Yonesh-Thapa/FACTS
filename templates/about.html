About Us

Learn more about Future Accountant Coaching and Training Services

Our Mission

At Future Accountant Coaching and Training Services (F.A.C.T.S), we are dedicated to empowering aspiring accountants with practical skills that bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world accounting practices. We believe in providing accessible, high-quality training that prepares our students for successful careers in the accounting industry.

Our 5-week intensive program is designed to give you hands-on experience with industry-standard accounting software, practical knowledge of tax regulations, and essential workplace skills that will make you stand out to potential employers.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert instruction from certified accountants
  • Hands-on training with Xero and MYOB
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering technical and soft skills
  • Career support and job placement assistance

Meet Our Lead Instructor

ASA CPA Xero Advisor Certified

Darshan Kumar Thapa

Program Director & Lead Instructor

With over 10 years of experience in accounting and financial management, Darshan brings practical industry knowledge to his teaching. As a certified CPA and Xero Advisor, he specializes in helping students master both the theoretical concepts and practical applications of modern accounting.

Darshan's teaching philosophy centers on providing students with real-world scenarios and hands-on experience that prepares them for immediate success in the workplace. His extensive network in the accounting industry also helps students connect with potential employers.

Our Core Values

Practical Education

We focus on teaching practical, job-ready skills that directly apply to today's accounting workplace, going beyond theoretical concepts to provide real-world applications.

Personalized Approach

We believe in the power of individualized attention and mentorship, maintaining small class sizes to ensure each student receives the support they need to succeed.

Industry Connections

We maintain strong relationships with accounting firms and businesses, helping our graduates find internships and job opportunities through our extensive professional network.

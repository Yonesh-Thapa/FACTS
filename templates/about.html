About F.A.C.T.S

Led by Darshan Kumar Thapa, ASA CPA, MPAS, Xero Certified Consultant

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic accounting education and practical workplace skills, empowering graduates to confidently enter the Australian accounting profession.

At Future Accountant Coaching and Training Services, we're committed to transforming accounting graduates into job-ready professionals through practical, hands-on training that addresses the real-world needs of accounting employers across Australia.

We believe that every accounting graduate deserves the opportunity to build the practical skills, software proficiency, and professional confidence required to launch a successful career, regardless of their location or background.

Through our specialized 8-week program, we combine technical accounting software training with essential career development skills, creating well-rounded professionals ready to make immediate contributions in the workplace.

Our Vision

To be Australia's leading training provider for accounting graduates, recognized for producing exceptionally well-prepared professionals who thrive in the modern accounting workplace.

We envision a future where every accounting graduate in Australia has access to high-quality, practical training that complements their academic education and prepares them to excel in the rapidly evolving accounting industry.

Our goal is to create a community of F.A.C.T.S alumni who are known in the industry for their practical skills, technological adaptability, and professional readiness—setting a new standard for entry-level accounting professionals.

We strive to continuously evolve our curriculum to incorporate emerging technologies and industry trends, ensuring our graduates are prepared not just for today's accounting roles, but for the future of the profession.

What We Stand For

Practical Excellence

We believe in hands-on learning that directly translates to workplace success. Our curriculum focuses on the practical skills and software proficiency that employers value most.

Personalized Support

We maintain small class sizes to ensure each student receives individualized attention. We recognize that every learner is unique and tailor our approach to meet diverse learning needs.

Innovation

We embrace emerging technologies and industry trends to ensure our training remains cutting-edge. Our curriculum evolves continuously to prepare graduates for the future of accounting.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all that we do. We believe in transparency, honesty, and accountability—values that are essential for success in the accounting profession.

Professional Growth

We're committed to nurturing not just technical skills, but the complete professional. Our training incorporates communication, workplace etiquette, and career development.

Accessibility

We believe quality accounting training should be accessible to graduates across Australia. Our 100% online delivery model ensures location is never a barrier to career advancement.

Our Story

F.A.C.T.S was founded by Darshan Kumar Thapa after recognizing a significant gap in the Australian accounting education system. While many graduates leave university with strong theoretical knowledge, they often lack the practical, software-based skills that employers seek in entry-level accountants. The program also develops essential soft skills such as communication, adaptability, and teamwork to support professional growth.

Drawing on his experience as both an accountant and educator, Darshan developed a comprehensive 8-week program specifically designed to bridge this gap and help recent graduates secure their first accounting roles.

With his extensive background in public practice accounting, cloud accounting solutions, tax compliance, and client advisory services, Darshan brings real-world expertise directly to his students. His approachable teaching style and passion for mentoring have made him highly regarded among graduates seeking to enhance their employability in Australia's competitive accounting job market.

Darshan personally guides each cohort through the entire 8-week program, providing hands-on instruction and mentorship. His teaching philosophy emphasizes practical skills that directly translate to workplace success, ensuring students not only understand accounting concepts but can apply them effectively in professional settings.

FACTS is committed to helping accounting graduates succeed. Our training not only builds technical expertise but also emphasizes soft skills that are critical in today's job market.

Our approach combines technical training with career development, ensuring that our graduates are not only proficient in industry-standard accounting software but also prepared to navigate the job search process effectively.

Calendly Integration Management

Automated with Calendly

Free sessions are now fully managed via Calendly, which provides the following benefits:

  • Automated scheduling - Users select their preferred time
  • Instant confirmations - No manual sending of links required
  • Automatic reminders - Attendees receive notifications before sessions
  • Calendar integration - Sessions appear in your calendar automatically
  • Reschedule option - Users can easily change their appointment if needed
